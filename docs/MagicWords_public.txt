Down below is a list of all the current Magic Words implemented on the OTP AI server with the exclusion of Staff-specific commmands and a example on how to use them.
Some Magic Words have aliases available for usage.

1.) ~maxbankmoney - Sets your bank to the maximum amount. (Amount: 12000)
2.) ~rich - Fills your jellybean jar to the maximum amount that your toon can hold. (Default: 40)
3.) ~maxtoon - Maxs your toons stats to end-game status.
4.) ~toonup - Heals your toon to your maximum laff.
5.) ~enabletpall - Grants your Toon teleport access to all the playgrounds.
6.) ~cogindex, ~setcogindex - Puts a Cog Suit on your Toon. (Example: ~cogindex 1 or setcogindex 1)
7.) ~name - Sets your toons name to the specified name. (Example: ~name Rocket)
8.) ~pinkslips - Gives you the specified amount of Pink Slips. (Example: ~pinkskips 1)
9.) ~tickets - Gives you the specified amount of Racing Tickets. (Example: ~tickets 1)
10.) ~newsummons - Gives you a specified amount of summons. (Example: ~newsummons 1 1)
11.) ~cogpagefull - Maxes your cog page.
12.) ~spawnfo - Spawns a Sellbot Field Office. (Example: ~spawnfo s)
13.) ~skipvp - Skips a part of the VP. (Example: ~skipvp next)
14.) ~nametagstyle - Sets your nametag style on your Toon. (Example: ~nametagstyle boardwalk)
15.) ~newcatalog, ~catalog, ~nextcatalog - Gives your toon the next catalog.
16.) ~setsillymeter, ~setsilly, ~sillymeter, ~sillymeterphase, ~sillyphase, ~phase, ~silly - Sets the Silly Meter phase to the specified value. (Example: ~setsilly 1)
17.) ~dauntless, ~ttplanetmeme, ~toontownotpmemes - Gives you a DNA string close to Dauntless from Toontown Planet.
18.) ~phone-task-skip, ~phone-skip - Skips the Clarabelle phone task.
19.) ~skipmovie, ~movieskip - Skips the movie of cog battle.
20.) ~friend-task-skip, ~friend-skip - Skip the friend task.
21.) ~fireworks - Enables fireworks. (Example: ~fireworks july4)
22.) ~maxdoodle - Maxes your doodle stats.
23.) ~restockinv, ~inventoryrestock - Restocks your inventory.
24.) ~clearinv, clearinventory - Clears your inventory.
25.) ~endmaze - Ends a Sellbot Field Office maze.
26.) ~setce, ~cheesyeffect - Sets a specified Cheesy Effect in a specified zone for a specified amount of time. (Example: ~setce 6 1 2000 - Sets Small Toon effect in Toontown Central.)
27.) ~growflowers - Grows your flowers in your Estate.
