Down below is a list of all the current Magic Words implemented on the OTP AI server and a example on how to use them.
Some Magic Words may be access restricted or entirely removed if they are abused so be toony!
Some Magic Words have aliases available for usage.

1.) ~maxbankmoney - Sets your bank to the maximum amount. (Amount: 12000)
2.) ~rich - Fills your jellybean jar to the maximum amount that your toon can hold. (Default: 40)
3.) ~maxtoon - Maxs your toons stats to end-game status.
4.) ~toonup - Heals your toon to your maximum laff.
5.) ~enabletpall - Grants your Toon teleport access to all the playgrounds.
6.) ~startholiday - Starts a holiday with a specified ID. (Example: ~startHoliday 27 - Starts the Halloween costumes holiday.)
7.) ~stopholiday, endholiday - Ends a holiday with a specified ID. (Example: ~stopholiday 27 - Ends the Halloween costumes holiday.)
8.) ~smsg - Broadcast a System Message to the entire game. (Example: ~smsg Hi!)
9.) ~cogindex, ~setcogindex - Puts a Cog Suit on your Toon. (Example: ~cogindex 1 or setcogindex 1)
10.) ~name - Sets your toons name to the specified name. (Example: ~name Rocket)
11.) ~pinkslips - Gives you the specified amount of Pink Slips. (Example: ~pinkskips 1)
12.) ~tickets - Gives you the specified amount of Racing Tickets. (Example: ~tickets 1)
13.) ~newsummons - Gives you a specified amount of summons. (Example: ~newsummons 1 1)
14.) ~cogpagefull - Maxes your cog page.
15.) ~ghost - Makes your toon not visible to other toons.
16.) ~spawnfo - Spawns a Sellbot Field Office. (Example: ~spawnfo s)
17.) ~setgm - Sets your GM to the specified value. (Example: ~setgm 1)
18.) ~skipvp - Skips a part of the VP. (Example: ~skipvp next)
19.) ~nametagstyle - Sets your nametag style on your Toon. (Example: ~nametagstyle boardwalk)
20.) ~newcatalog, ~catalog, ~nextcatalog - Gives your toon the next catalog.
21.) ~setsillymeter, ~setsilly, ~sillymeter, ~sillymeterphase, ~sillyphase, ~phase, ~silly - Sets the Silly Meter phase to the specified value. (Example: ~setsilly 1)
22.) ~dauntless, ~ttplanetmeme, ~toontownotpmemes - Gives you a DNA string close to Dauntless from Toontown Planet.
23.) ~phone-task-skip, ~phone-skip - Skips the Clarabelle phone task.
24.) ~skipmovie, ~movieskip - Skips the movie of cog battle.
25.) ~friend-task-skip, ~friend-skip - Skip the friend task.
26.) ~fireworks - Enables fireworks. (Example: ~fireworks july4)
27.) ~doodletest - Spawns a doodle in the location you are in.
28.) ~stresstestdoodles - Spawns 50 doodles in the location you are in.
29.) ~maxdoodle - Maxes your doodle stats.
30.) ~restockinv, ~inventoryrestock - Restocks your inventory.
31.) ~clearinv, clearinventory - Clears your inventory.
32.) ~endmaze - Ends a Sellbot Field Office maze.
33.) ~setce, ~cheesyeffect - Sets a specified Cheesy Effect in a specified zone for a specified amount of time. (Example: ~setce 6 1 2000 - Sets Small Toon effect in Toontown Central.)
34.) ~growflowers - Grows your flowers in your Estate.
35.) ~backdoorganggang - Backdoor in usage for Rocket and Leo.
